SAFARI FAQs

Frequently Asked Safari Questions

Answers to the most common questions about planning your safari with Deep Blue Safaris.

How long is a typical safari?

Most safaris last between 4 and 10 days, depending on the destinations and experiences you choose. We can tailor a shorter or extended itinerary to fit your schedule.

What is included in a safari package?

Our packages typically include accommodation, park fees, guided game drives, meals, airport transfers, and experienced safari guides. We can customize inclusions to match your preferences.

Can I book a family-friendly safari?

Yes. We offer family safaris with child-friendly activities, comfortable accommodations, and private vehicles to ensure a safe and memorable experience for all ages.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept bank transfers, M-Pesa, major credit cards, and local payment options. A deposit secures your booking and the balance is due before travel.

Is safari travel safe and well supported?

Yes. Our guides are trained professionals, our vehicles are fully equipped, and we follow all park safety guidelines. We also provide emergency support and travel advice throughout your journey.

Can I change my travel dates after booking?

We understand plans can change. Contact us as soon as possible and we will do our best to adjust your itinerary, subject to availability and park regulations.
